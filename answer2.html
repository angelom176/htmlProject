Where can I see my courses?

To view your courses, log in to your account and navigate to the "My Courses" section in the main menu. Here, you will find a list of all the courses you are enrolled in.

If you have any issues accessing your courses, please contact our support team for assistance.

For more information, you can also check our FAQ section.
