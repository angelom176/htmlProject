How can I update my account information?

To update your account information, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the "Account Settings" section.
  3. Update your information as needed.
  4. Click "Save Changes" to apply the updates.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team for assistance.
